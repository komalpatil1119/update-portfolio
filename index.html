About Me

Driven by a deep love for nature and inspired by the masters, I create realistic art that brings the beauty of life to life with every brushstroke.

Chandrashekhar Prabhakar Aher

I love to paint nature and all beautiful things around us , From the early childhood I liked to draw different types of drawings and coloring nature's objects . At the first school years I always paint beautiful paintings , always loves to paint realistic and beautiful things in the nature I was always inspired by the old masters painting,, I trys to learn from great old masters ,, I will develope my observations ,, coloring applications close to natural and realistic feel , " ART IS LIFE "
