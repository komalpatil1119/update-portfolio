About Me

Driven by a deep love for nature and inspired by the masters, I create realistic art that brings the beauty of life to life with every brushstroke.

It sounds like Chandrashekhar Prabhakar Aher has a deep passion for painting, especially focused on nature and its beauty. His love for realism and attention to detail is evident from an early age, along with his admiration for the old masters of art. The phrase “ART IS LIFE” reflects his belief in the essential connection between life and the artistic expression of it.
